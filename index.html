Meet My Family

The Cermaks

In August 1969, our parents, Sharon and Laird Cermak got married! They went on to create a beautiful family together with 4 amazing children - Kendra, the oldest, Mike, the only boy, Bethany aka Betra, and Amanda, the youngest. Kendra married Dan and they have 3 amazing boys together, Parker, Lincoln and Hudson. Bethany married Eric rounding out our family of 10 (and counting!)

We love traveling together and have created this website to share our adventures, videos, photos, memories and more!

Thank you so much to the matriarch of the family Sharon Cermak for making this all possible and bringing us together as a family every few years!
