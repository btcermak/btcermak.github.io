Sedona Makes Me 'Happy'

2014 Cermak Family Vacation

In August 2014, my family traveled from the far corners of the globe to play amoungst the breath-taking views of Sedona, AZ.

While there we enjoyed hiking, biking, swimming, hot tubbing, hot air ballooning and clapping along to 'Happy' by Pharrell Williams.
