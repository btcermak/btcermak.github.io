You Better Belize It

2010 Cermak Family Vacation

In 2010, my family traveled from the far corners of the globe to play in the waters off Misquito Cove in Belize.

While there we enjoyed snorkeling, scuba diving, surving a tropical storm, swimming, drinking, feeding sharks and more!

Thank you so much to the matriarch of the family Sharon Cermak for making this all possible and giving us a lifetime of memories!
