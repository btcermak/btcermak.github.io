Dude Ranch in Idaho

2012 Cermak Family Vacation

In July 2012, my family traveled from the far corners of the globe for some horse play at Red Horse Mountain Ranch in Idaho.

While there we enjoyed horseback riding, archery, swimming, white water rafting, Dude Ranching and, of course, choreographing moves to 'Call Me Maybe' by Carly Rae Jepsen.

Thank you so much to the matriarch of the family Sharon Cermak for making this all possible and for all the unforgetable memories.
